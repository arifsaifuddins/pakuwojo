ABOUT

This Pakuwojo organization was formed about 7 years ago, and the establishment of this organization to connect the relationship between Javanese people, to make it more orderly.


Pakuwojo is located in Khartoum, Sudan, close to International University of Africa. and the population of Pakuwojo member is from among students, workers, and some people who has have lived long in Java.
